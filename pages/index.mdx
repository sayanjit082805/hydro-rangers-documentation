# Introduction

Welcome to the documentation of our project! Our project strives to provide a low-cost and a sustainable means of controlling water-levels in water tanks in residential homes.

## Target sustainable development goals

This project aims to contribute to the following sustainable development goals:
- [SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation](https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/water-and-sanitation/)
- [SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure](https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/infrastructure-industrialization/)
- [SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities](https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/cities/)
- [SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production](https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/sustainable-consumption-production/)
